About Us

Genesis is an all-inclusive program that will make you an astronaut in less than two years. No degree required! At $12 million, our program is the least expensive, highest quality astronaut training program on the market. The program includes: an official astronaut suit, 16 months of training, 2 flights, 3 meals a day on said flights, and much, much more!

Our History

The first stages of Genesis began in 2023, before commercial space travel was a widely accepted fact of life. Our project was not always smooth sailing. There were some disagreements, funding issues, resource availability issues, and people just being skeptical. After all, how could something like this ever be a reality? I'll be honest, when this idea was born, I didn't think it would be possible either, but here we are. So, enjoy the Genesis experience, and know that without you, this would have never been possible.
