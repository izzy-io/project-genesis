Pre-Flight Basics

Here at Genesis, we take pride in the preparation that goes into the execution of our programs. We extensively plan every piece of the process, from the day you sign up to the recovery of our last flight. You'll be in great hands. Training will begin on February 1st, 2032. A full course plan will be available on-site of the first training session.

Procedures

We have a very extensive list of precautions that we must take and procedures that must be performed in order to ensure your safety. These precautions include running several tests on ships before takeoff, running several tests on the autopilot systems, and giving astronauts exams to ensure they know how to operate the ship in the case of an emergency. Procedures include, but are not limited to, pre-flight safety briefs, on-flight daily check-ins, and a certified staff member on every flight, at all times.
