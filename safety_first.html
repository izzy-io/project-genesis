Safety First

Basics

Here at Genesis, safety is our first priority. To ensure our astronauts come home safely, we only use the best of the best in safety mechanisms, at the base and in space! Our state-of-the-art technolgy has been tested by astronauts far and wide. You will receive weekly safety meetings and a safety brief before each new concept is introduced. By the end pf our training, you will feel as if you born to be in space.

Testing

If you have any experience with anything, you know that projects like these take lots of testing and correcting. However, we never test on humans until the final stages. What does this mean? You're not the first person to step on our ships, but you are not far behind. Our ships are perfected by test dummies before they are tested on humans. We value human life more than anything else in the world or out of it.
