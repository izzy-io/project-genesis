Ready, Set, Launch!

Included Flights

There will be two flights included in your training. Included launches are set for May 27th, 2033 and June 10th, 2033. Flights will last for seven days each, with meals and bedding included. There will be 7 astronauts on each flight, with 1-2 certified staff members present. More details will be available when you sign up and place your down payment.

Additional Flights

Additional flight dates have yet to be set, due to the fact that we do not know many astronauts will be attending each flight. Resources, precautions, and procedures will be the same as the included flights. Prices will range between $1 million and $1.5 million per flight.
