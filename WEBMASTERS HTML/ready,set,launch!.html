Ready, Set, Launch!

Included Flights

There will be two flights included in your training. Included launches are set for May 27th, 2033 and June 10th, 2033. Flights will last for seven days each, with meals and bedding included. There will be 7 astronauts on each flight, with 1-2 certified staff members present. More details will be available when you purchase your chosen package.

Additional Flights

Additional flight dates have been set for June 30th, July 15th, August 1st, and August 16th. You can purchase one flight or two flights. Prices will be available when you click on the hyperlinks. Each flight will include the standard features included with the flights included in the packages. You will not need to pay extra for the space suit unless you need a new one or choose to buy the suit instead.
